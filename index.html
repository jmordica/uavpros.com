Tactical Support

Buffer zones for criminal and drug seizures. Infrared for night-time detection. Grid searches made easier than ever. Why deploy noisy and costly helicopters when our stealthy drones can assist you at a fraction of the cost?

Search & Rescue

This is a ground-breaking arena for drones, and it’s proving to be a major life-saving technology. Drones can circle ahead of search parties, drop off supplies to locations inaccessible by ambulance, and traverse dangers zones that are too precarious for authorities.

Industrial

Meet the complex demands of industrial inspections by examining and sharing your aerial data. With adaptive capabilities and powerful command, our drones solve inspection issues from a birds-eye view.

Aerial Survey

The Agriculture and Forestry industries have adopted drone technology as a way for drastically cutting down on surveying man-hours. Industry experts have found that satellite technology cannot produce the same accurate and real-time captures that UAVs can provide.
